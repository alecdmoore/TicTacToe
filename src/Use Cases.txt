Use Cases:


Use Case: Starting a game:
1. Player selects a style by clicking corresponding button
2. Gameboard loads in


Use case: Making a move:
1. Player clicks tile on board
2. X/O moves to tile
3. Next player’s turn starts
Variation #1
1.1         After step 2, player hits undo button 
1.2         Last tile placed is removed
1.3         Continue to step 1
Variation #2
2.1        Player hits undos move 3 times in a row
2.2        Player cannot undo anymore
Variation #3
3.1        Player makes winning move
3.2        ‘Start new game’ button appears
